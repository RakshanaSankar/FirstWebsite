Hello, I'm Pikachu

I am a Pokemon.

I am the world-renowned Mouse Pokémon and the official mascot of the Pokémon franchise. Recognizable by its bright yellow fur and lightning-bolt tail, it captures electricity in the red pouches on its cheeks. Since its debut in 1996, it has become a global cultural icon, symbolizing friendship and adventure.

About Me

Who Am I ?

I’m a small Mouse Pokémon with a huge spark! I might look cute and cuddly, but my red cheeks are actually rechargeable batteries that store powerful electricity. When I get fired up or sense danger, I release a crackling Thunderbolt to protect my friends. I’m a social creature who loves to travel. When I meet my friends, we press our tails together to share a little 'zip' of energy—it’s like a high-five, but way more shocking!

What I love ?

  • Sweet Berries: Especially when they are roasted just right with a tiny zap.
  • Ketchup: Don't ask why... I just really, really love the bottle.
  • Adventure: I’m always ready to explore new regions and meet new trainers.

My Electric Cheeks ⚡

My red pouches aren't just for show! They are my power source where I store electricity. If you see them flickering, it means I’m ready to unleash a Thunderbolt!

My Iconic Tail

Check out my lightning-bolt tail! It’s not just my signature look—I use it to sense my surroundings and even discharge extra energy into the ground when I'm overcharged.

Global Mascot Status 🌏

I’ve traveled from the Kanto region to every corner of the world. Whether it’s in games, movies, or your new website, I’m the spark that brings people together!
